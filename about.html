About Us

The University of Eastern Pangasinan is committed to providing quality education and developing students into competent, responsible, and globally competitive professionals. The institution continuously promotes excellence, discipline, leadership, and innovation for the betterment of society.

Mission

To provide accessible and quality education that empowers students through academic excellence, leadership, and moral values.

Vision

To become one of the leading educational institutions recognized for producing globally competitive graduates and responsible citizens.

Goals

To develop students with strong academic foundation, professional competence, leadership skills, and commitment to community service and nation building.
